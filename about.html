About the Creators

GHT Productions was founded by Derek and Jason on April 13, 2016.
It originally posted pranks and tutorials, but soon moved on to multiple
games, and finally settling on Diep.io.
[GHT]LIGAR joined the team on May 27, 2016 and made his debut with
the most viewed video on GHT Productions at the time, showcasing his
homemade crossbow. Jason, Derek and Ligar have worked together
on GHT Productions to grow it into the channel it is today. As of now,
they have over 4500 subscribers and 1.5 million total views.
